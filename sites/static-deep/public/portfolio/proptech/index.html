PropTech Case Study

Client: PropTech

Industry: Real Estate

Service: Property Management Platform

Result: 10K properties managed

Challenge

PropTech approached us with a critical need to modernize their digital infrastructure. They were facing scalability issues, outdated technology, and increasing user demands that their existing system couldn't handle.

Solution

We designed and implemented a comprehensive property management platform solution tailored to real estate industry requirements. Our approach included:

  • In-depth discovery and requirements analysis
  • Modern, scalable architecture design
  • Agile development with regular stakeholder feedback
  • Comprehensive testing and quality assurance
  • Smooth deployment and team training

Technology Stack

We leveraged cutting-edge technologies including TypeScript, React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Redis, and deployed on Cloudflare Workers for global edge performance.

Results

The project delivered exceptional results: 10K properties managed. Beyond the metrics, PropTech gained a robust, maintainable platform that positions them for future growth.
