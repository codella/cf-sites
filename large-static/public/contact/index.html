Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

It varies based on scope. Small websites might take 4-6 weeks, while complex applications can span several months. We'll provide realistic estimates after understanding your requirements.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely. We've worked with companies at every stage, from pre-seed startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. We tailor our engagement model to fit your situation.

What's your pricing model?

We typically work on a project basis with fixed pricing, though we also offer retainer arrangements for ongoing work. We'll discuss options that make sense for your needs.

Can you work with our existing team?

Yes. We often embed with client teams, whether leading projects or augmenting existing capabilities. Collaboration and knowledge transfer are priorities in every engagement.
