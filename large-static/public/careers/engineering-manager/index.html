About the Role

We're looking for an exceptional Engineering Manager to join our growing team. In this role, you'll work on challenging problems, collaborate with talented colleagues, and make a significant impact on our product and customers.

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams

  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication abilities
  • Experience with modern development tools and practices
  • Passion for technology and continuous learning

  • Generous PTO and parental leave
